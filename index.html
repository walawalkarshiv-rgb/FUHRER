About Me

Where India Meets Berlin

I grew up in India and moved to Berlin to pursue my Master's in International Management — and somewhere along the way I became fluent in both corporate German culture and the warm, relationship-driven style of Indian workplaces.

That bridge between two very different professional worlds is something I bring into everything I do in HR. I believe great hiring isn't just about matching skills — it's about understanding people, their context, and where they'll genuinely thrive.

Outside of HR, I'm a co-founder, a logistics worker who shows up before sunrise, a photographer who chases golden hour in Sächsische Schweiz, and someone who believes the best conversations happen over chai — or a good film.
